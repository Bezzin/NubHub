# Acne in Pregnancy: Does It Predict Gender?

The old wives' tale says acne during pregnancy means you're having a girl (she's 'stealing your beauty'). Does it work?

## Table of Contents

1. [The Theory](#s1)
2. [The Reality](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## The Theory {#s1}

More acne = girl (the theory says girls steal maternal beauty). Clear skin = boy.

## The Reality {#s2}

Pregnancy acne is caused by hormonal changes, specifically increased androgen levels. It affects both boy and girl pregnancies equally. Accuracy: approximately 50%.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Does pregnancy acne mean I'm having a girl?

No. Pregnancy acne is caused by hormonal changes and occurs in both boy and girl pregnancies. This is an old wives' tale with no scientific basis.
