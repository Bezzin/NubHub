# Bump Shape and Gender: Does It Predict Boy or Girl?

Round bump = girl, pointed bump = boy — or so the old wives' tale says. Here's the truth.

## Table of Contents

1. [The Theory](#s1)
2. [The Reality](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## The Theory {#s1}

A round, wide bump = girl. A pointed, forward-pointing bump = boy.

## The Reality {#s2}

Bump shape is determined by your body shape, muscle tone, abdominal structure, and baby's position. It has nothing to do with gender. Accuracy: approximately 50%.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Does bump shape predict gender?

No. Bump shape is determined by your body structure and baby's position, not gender. This old wives' tale is approximately 50% accurate.
