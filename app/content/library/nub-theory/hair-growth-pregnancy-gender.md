# Hair Growth in Pregnancy: Does It Predict Gender?

The tale says faster hair growth means boy, and thinner hair means girl. Is there any truth?

## Table of Contents

1. [The Theory](#s1)
2. [The Reality](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## The Theory {#s1}

Thicker, faster-growing hair = boy. Thinner or unchanged hair = girl.

## The Reality {#s2}

Pregnancy hormones (oestrogen) extend the hair growth phase for all women, regardless of baby's sex. Most pregnant women experience thicker hair. Accuracy: approximately 50%.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Does hair growth predict baby gender?

No. Pregnancy hormones cause thicker hair in most women regardless of baby's sex. This old wives' tale has no scientific basis.
