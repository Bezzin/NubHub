# Surgical Management of Miscarriage (ERPC)

Surgical management (ERPC or SMM) is a minor operation to remove pregnancy tissue from the uterus. It's done under general or local anaesthetic.

## What to Expect

The procedure takes 10-15 minutes under anaesthetic. A small tube is used to remove the tissue. You can usually go home the same day. Bleeding afterwards is lighter than with medical management. Physical recovery is quick (1-2 days) but emotional recovery takes longer.

## FAQ

### Is ERPC painful?

The procedure itself isn't painful because you're under anaesthetic. Afterwards, you may have mild cramping (like a period) for 1-2 days. Paracetamol is usually sufficient.

### How long is recovery after ERPC?

Physical recovery is 1-2 days. Bleeding lasts 1-2 weeks (lighter than medical management). You can return to work after 2-3 days. Emotional recovery takes longer.
