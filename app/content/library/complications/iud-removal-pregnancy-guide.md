# Iud Removal Pregnancy Guide

What happens if you get pregnant with an IUD? IUD removal in pregnancy guide.

## Overview

Getting pregnant with an IUD is rare but possible. Here's what happens.

## FAQ

### If you become pregnant with an IUD in place, your doctor will recommend removing it if possible. Leaving the IUD in place increases the risk of miscarriage, preterm birth, and infection. Removal in early pregnancy is usually straightforward but carries a small miscarriage risk. If the IUD can't be removed (e.g., strings are not visible), you'll be monitored closely.

Can I get pregnant with an IUD?

### Yes, although it's rare (failure rate is less than 1%). If you do get pregnant with an IUD, see your GP immediately to discuss removal and check for ectopic pregnancy.
