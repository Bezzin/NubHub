# Sudden Symptom Loss Pregnancy Get Checked

If pregnancy symptoms suddenly disappear, should you get checked? Guide to symptom loss.

## Overview

A sudden loss of pregnancy symptoms can be alarming. Here's when to get checked.

## FAQ

### Gradual symptom loss as you approach 12 weeks is normal (hCG levels decline). However, SUDDEN loss of all symptoms (overnight) warrants a check — especially if you previously had strong symptoms. Contact your midwife or EPU for a scan. In many cases, the pregnancy is fine, but it's always best to check.

Is it normal for pregnancy symptoms to suddenly disappear?

### Gradual loss is normal as you approach 12-14 weeks. Sudden loss (overnight) of all symptoms is less common and warrants a check. Contact your midwife for a scan to confirm everything is OK.
