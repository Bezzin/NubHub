# How to Cope After a Miscarriage

Miscarriage is emotionally devastating. Here's how to cope with the physical and emotional recovery.

## Physical Recovery

Bleeding lasts 1-3 weeks depending on the management method. Your hormones will take 4-6 weeks to return to normal. You may experience mood swings, breast tenderness, and fatigue as hormones drop. Your period should return in 4-6 weeks.

## Emotional Recovery

Allow yourself to grieve. Everyone copes differently — there's no right or wrong way. Talk to your partner, friends, or a counsellor. Join a support group. Don't rush trying again — wait until you feel ready emotionally. The Miscarriage Association offers excellent support.

## FAQ

### How long should I wait before trying again?

Physically, you can try again after your next period (4-6 weeks). Emotionally, wait until you feel ready. There's no medical reason to wait longer after a single miscarriage.

### When will I feel normal again?

Physical recovery takes 1-2 weeks. Emotional recovery varies — some women feel better in weeks, others take months. Both are normal. Seek support if you're struggling.
