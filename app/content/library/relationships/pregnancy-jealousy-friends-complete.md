# Pregnancy Jealousy Friends Complete

Pregnancy jealousy between friends — why it happens and how to navigate it.

## Overview

Pregnancy can trigger jealousy between friends. Here's how to handle it sensitively.

## FAQ

### Jealousy can arise when: one friend is pregnant and another is struggling to conceive, one friend is pregnant and another has decided not to have children, or there's perceived competition about timing. These feelings are valid and common. Communication, empathy, and space are key.

What if my friend is jealous of my pregnancy?

### Be sensitive. Don't complain about pregnancy symptoms to someone struggling to conceive. Give them space if they need it. Understand that their jealousy doesn't mean they're not happy for you — they're processing their own emotions.
