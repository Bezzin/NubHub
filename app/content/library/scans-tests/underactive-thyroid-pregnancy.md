# Underactive Thyroid Pregnancy

Underactive thyroid (hypothyroidism) in pregnancy — symptoms, treatment, and why it matters.

## Overview

An underactive thyroid during pregnancy needs monitoring and treatment. Here's what you need to know.

## FAQ

### Hypothyroidism in pregnancy can affect baby's development if untreated. Symptoms include fatigue, weight gain, and cold sensitivity — which overlap with normal pregnancy symptoms. Treatment is with levothyroxine, which is safe in pregnancy. Your thyroid levels will be monitored regularly.

How is underactive thyroid treated in pregnancy?

### With levothyroxine tablets, which are safe in pregnancy. Your dose may need to increase during pregnancy. Blood tests monitor your levels every 4-6 weeks.
