# Protein In Urine Pregnancy

Protein in urine during pregnancy — what it means, when to worry, and how it's monitored.

## Overview

Finding protein in your urine during pregnancy can be concerning. Here's what it means.

## FAQ

### Small amounts of protein can be normal in pregnancy. Higher levels may indicate: pre-eclampsia (a serious condition involving high blood pressure), kidney infection, or urinary tract infection. Your midwife monitors protein at every visit.

Is protein in urine always serious?

### No. A trace amount is common. Your midwife will track the level over time. Rising protein with high blood pressure needs urgent assessment.
