# Private Early Scan: Is It Worth It?

Many women consider a private early scan before their NHS 12-week scan. Here's what to consider.

## Pros of a Private Early Scan

- Reassurance - seeing a heartbeat reduces anxiety
- Earlier than the NHS 12-week scan
- More time with the sonographer
- You may get photos and video

## Cons

- Cost: £60-£200
- May be too early to see much (before 6-7 weeks)
- Insurance usually doesn't cover it

## FAQ

### How much does a private early scan cost?

Private early pregnancy scans cost between £60 and £200 in the UK, depending on the clinic and location.

### When is the best time for a private scan?

7-8 weeks is ideal for a private early scan. At this point you can see the heartbeat and confirm the pregnancy is viable.
