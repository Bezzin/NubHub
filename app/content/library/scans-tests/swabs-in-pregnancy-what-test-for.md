# Swabs In Pregnancy What Test For

What do swabs in pregnancy test for? Complete guide to vaginal swabs during pregnancy.

## Overview

You may have swabs taken during pregnancy. Here's what they test for.

## FAQ

### Swabs may test for: Group B strep (GBS, tested later in pregnancy), chlamydia, gonorrhoea, bacterial vaginosis (BV), and thrush. Some are routine; others are done if you have symptoms.

When are swabs taken in pregnancy?

### At your booking appointment, you may be offered screening for infections. Group B strep is typically tested at 35-37 weeks. Symptom-based swabs can be done anytime.
