# Chlamydia Screening Pregnancy

Chlamydia screening in pregnancy — who gets tested, why it matters, and treatment options.

## Overview

Chlamydia screening is offered as part of routine antenatal care in many areas. Here's what you need to know.

## FAQ

### Chlamydia is a common STI that can cause complications in pregnancy including preterm birth and eye infections in the baby. Screening is offered at the booking appointment in many areas. If positive, it's easily treated with pregnancy-safe antibiotics.

Is chlamydia screening routine in pregnancy?

### In many areas, yes. It's offered at your booking appointment. Even if it's not offered routinely, you can request it.
