# Empty Sac at 7 Weeks: What Happens Next?

An empty sac at 7 weeks is more concerning than at 6 weeks but may still be a dating issue. Here's what happens next.

## What It Means

At 7 weeks, you would expect to see a fetal pole and possibly a heartbeat. An empty sac could mean:
- Your dates are wrong and you're earlier than 7 weeks
- The pregnancy has stopped developing (missed miscarriage or blighted ovum)

## What Happens Next

You'll have a follow-up scan in 1-2 weeks. If the sac is still empty at that point, a diagnosis of miscarriage may be made.

## FAQ

### How common is an empty sac at 7 weeks?

It happens in a significant number of early scans. Many cases are simply due to incorrect dating. A follow-up scan is essential before any diagnosis.
