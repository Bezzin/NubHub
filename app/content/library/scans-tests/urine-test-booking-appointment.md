# Urine Test Booking Appointment

What does the urine test at your booking appointment check for? Complete guide to pregnancy urine tests.

## Overview

Your midwife will test your urine at every antenatal appointment. Here's what they're checking for.

## FAQ

### The urine test checks for: protein (which can indicate pre-eclampsia), glucose (which can indicate gestational diabetes), leukocytes (sign of infection), nitrites (sign of bacterial infection), and blood (which can indicate UTI or other issues). You'll provide a sample at every appointment.

What does protein in urine mean?

### A small amount of protein can be normal in pregnancy. High levels may indicate pre-eclampsia, a serious condition. Your midwife will monitor levels at every appointment.
