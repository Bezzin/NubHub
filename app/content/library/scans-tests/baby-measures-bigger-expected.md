# What If Baby Measures Bigger Than Expected?

If your baby measures bigger than expected at your scan, here's what it means.

## Why Baby Might Measure Big

- Your dates are wrong (you're further along than you thought)
- Large but healthy baby (normal variation)
- In later pregnancy, gestational diabetes can cause large babies

## What Happens Next

If baby measures big, the sonographer will adjust your due date based on scan measurements. In most cases, this is simply a dating adjustment.

## FAQ

### Does measuring big mean I'll need to be induced?

Not in the first trimester. Measuring big early on usually just means your dates need adjusting. Decisions about induction happen later in pregnancy.
