# Nail Changes in Pregnancy: Complete Guide

Pregnancy hormones can affect your nails too. Here's what to expect.

## Table of Contents

1. [Common Changes](#s1)
2. [Why It Happens](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Common Changes {#s1}

Many women notice their nails grow faster and are stronger during pregnancy. However, some experience brittle, soft, or peeling nails. Both are normal.

## Why It Happens {#s2}

Increased oestrogen and nutrients can strengthen nails. However, hormonal shifts can also make nails more brittle. Changes are temporary.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Will my nails go back to normal after pregnancy?

Yes. Nail changes during pregnancy are temporary and caused by hormones. Your nails will return to normal within a few months after birth.
