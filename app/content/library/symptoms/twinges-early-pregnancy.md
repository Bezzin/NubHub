# Twinges Early Pregnancy

Twinges in early pregnancy — what causes them and when to worry.

## Overview

Feeling twinges in your lower abdomen during early pregnancy? Here's what they mean.

## FAQ

### Twinges are quick, sharp sensations that last only a second or two. They're usually caused by: round ligament stretching, your uterus growing, gas, or the corpus luteum. They're very common and usually nothing to worry about.

Are twinges normal in early pregnancy?

### Yes. Quick, sharp twinges are common and usually caused by ligaments stretching. As long as they're brief and not accompanied by bleeding, they're normal.
