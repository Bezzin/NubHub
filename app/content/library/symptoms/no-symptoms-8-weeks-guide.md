# No Symptoms at 8 Weeks: What It Means

By 8 weeks, most women have noticeable symptoms. But some don't, and that can be normal.

## Table of Contents

1. [Is It Normal?](#s1)
2. [When to Worry](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Is It Normal? {#s1}

Some women never get strong pregnancy symptoms. If you've had a scan showing a heartbeat, no symptoms is not concerning. If you haven't had a scan, consider booking one for reassurance.

## When to Worry {#s2}

If you HAD symptoms and they suddenly disappeared, contact your midwife. But never having had symptoms is different from losing them.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is no symptoms at 8 weeks normal?

It can be. Some women have minimal symptoms. If you're worried, a scan can confirm the baby is OK. The presence or absence of symptoms doesn't reliably predict pregnancy health.
