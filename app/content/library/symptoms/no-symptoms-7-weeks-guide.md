# No Symptoms at 7 Weeks: Is Baby OK?

By 7 weeks, most women have some symptoms. But having none doesn't mean something is wrong.

## Table of Contents

1. [Why No Symptoms at 7 Weeks](#s1)
2. [Reassurance](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Why No Symptoms at 7 Weeks {#s1}

- Some women have very mild symptoms they don't notice
- Hormone levels may be normal but you're less sensitive
- Your body may handle pregnancy hormones well

## Reassurance {#s2}

The best way to know your baby is OK is a scan. A heartbeat at 7-8 weeks reduces miscarriage risk to less than 5%.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Can I have a healthy pregnancy with no symptoms at 7 weeks?

Yes. Some women have minimal symptoms throughout and have healthy babies. If you're worried, book a scan to see the heartbeat.
