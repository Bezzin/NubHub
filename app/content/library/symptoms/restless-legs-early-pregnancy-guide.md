# Restless Legs in Early Pregnancy

Restless leg syndrome (RLS) affects up to 20% of pregnant women. Here's how to manage it.

## Table of Contents

1. [What RLS Feels Like](#s1)
2. [Management](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## What RLS Feels Like {#s1}

An irresistible urge to move your legs, usually in the evening or at night. Uncomfortable sensations like crawling, tingling, or itching in your legs.

## Management {#s2}

Regular exercise, stretch before bed, warm baths, massage, reduce caffeine, and check your iron levels (anaemia can worsen RLS).

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is restless leg syndrome common in pregnancy?

Yes, up to 20% of pregnant women experience RLS, especially in the third trimester. It can start earlier for some women. Check your iron levels as deficiency can worsen it.
