# Does No Symptoms Mean Miscarriage?

Many women worry that having no pregnancy symptoms means something is wrong. In most cases, it doesn't.

## Table of Contents

1. [Why Some Women Have No Symptoms](#s1)
2. [What Matters](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Why Some Women Have No Symptoms {#s1}

- Hormone levels may be rising normally without causing symptoms
- Some women are less sensitive to hormonal changes
- Body may adjust more easily
- Symptoms may develop later

## What Matters {#s2}

The presence or absence of symptoms doesn't predict pregnancy health. Many women with no symptoms have healthy babies. Many women with severe symptoms have miscarriages. The only way to know is through scans.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Should I worry if I have no pregnancy symptoms?

Not necessarily. About 20% of women have minimal symptoms in early pregnancy. If you've seen a heartbeat on scan, miscarriage risk is low regardless of symptoms.

### What if my symptoms suddenly disappear?

If symptoms disappear suddenly after being strong, contact your midwife for reassurance. A scan can confirm the baby is OK. But gradual symptom reduction as you approach 12 weeks is normal.
