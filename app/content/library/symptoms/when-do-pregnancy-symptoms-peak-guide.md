# When Do Pregnancy Symptoms Peak Guide

When do pregnancy symptoms reach their peak? Guide to the worst weeks of the first trimester.

## Overview

Wondering when you'll feel your worst? Here's when pregnancy symptoms peak.

## FAQ

### Pregnancy symptoms typically peak between weeks 8-10. This is when hCG levels are highest. You may experience: maximum nausea, extreme fatigue, strongest food aversions, and peak breast tenderness. The good news: symptoms usually start easing from week 10-12 onwards.

What weeks are pregnancy symptoms the worst?

### Weeks 8-10 are typically the worst. hCG peaks during this time, causing maximum nausea, fatigue, and other symptoms. Most women start feeling better from week 12.
