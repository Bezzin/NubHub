# Pregnancy Doom Scrolling How Stop

Can't stop Googling pregnancy symptoms and horror stories? How to break the doom-scrolling habit.

## Overview

Doom-scrolling in pregnancy is a common anxiety behaviour. Here's how to break the habit.

## FAQ

### Doom-scrolling — compulsively searching for pregnancy problems online — feeds anxiety rather than relieving it. To stop: set limits on phone use, delete or hide news apps, replace scrolling with a positive activity, talk to someone instead of Googling, and remember that Dr Google is not your friend.

Why do I doom-scroll in pregnancy?

### It's an anxiety response — you're seeking reassurance through information. But reading horror stories makes anxiety worse. Each negative story reinforces your fears, even though statistically these outcomes are rare.
