# Symptoms Coming and Going in Early Pregnancy

If your pregnancy symptoms come and go, you're not alone. This is one of the most common concerns in early pregnancy.

## Table of Contents

1. [Why Symptoms Fluctuate](#s1)
2. [Is It Normal?](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Why Symptoms Fluctuate {#s1}

- Hormone levels rise in waves, not steadily
- Different days have different hCG levels
- Your body adjusts and readjusts
- Stress, diet, and sleep affect how you feel

## Is It Normal? {#s2}

Yes. Symptoms coming and going is completely normal. One day you might feel terribly nauseous, the next you feel fine. This doesn't mean something is wrong.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is it normal for pregnancy symptoms to come and go?

Yes. Hormone levels fluctuate, so symptoms vary day to day. One day of feeling fine doesn't mean your pregnancy is in trouble. This is one of the most common and normal experiences in early pregnancy.
