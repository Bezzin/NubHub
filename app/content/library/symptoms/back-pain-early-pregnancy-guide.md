# Back Pain in Early Pregnancy

Back pain can start surprisingly early in pregnancy. Here's what's causing it and what helps.

## Table of Contents

1. [Causes](#s1)
2. [Relief](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Causes {#s1}

Hormones (relaxin) loosen your ligaments, your centre of gravity shifts as your uterus grows, and stress can cause muscle tension. All contribute to back pain in early pregnancy.

## Relief {#s2}

Warm baths, gentle stretching, pregnancy yoga, good posture, supportive shoes, a pregnancy pillow, and paracetamol if needed. Avoid heavy lifting.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is back pain normal at 6 weeks pregnant?

Yes. Hormonal changes start affecting your back very early. Mild back pain is normal. Severe back pain, especially with bleeding, needs medical attention.

### Can I use a heat pad for back pain in pregnancy?

Yes, a warm (not hot) heat pad on your back is safe in pregnancy. Avoid hot tubs and saunas which can raise your core temperature too high.
