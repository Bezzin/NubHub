# Early Pregnancy Anxiety When To Get Help

When pregnancy anxiety becomes too much. Signs you need professional help and where to turn.

## Overview

Some anxiety in pregnancy is normal, but when does it become too much? Here's when to get help.

## FAQ

### Signs you may need professional help: anxiety is affecting your daily life, you can't sleep or eat, you're having panic attacks, you're constantly scanning for problems, you can't enjoy the pregnancy, or you're having intrusive thoughts. Tell your GP or midwife — they can refer you to perinatal mental health services.

Is anxiety in pregnancy normal?

### Yes. Some anxiety is completely normal — you're going through a major life change. But if anxiety is overwhelming, affecting your daily life, or causing panic attacks, it's time to get help.
