# No Symptoms at 6 Weeks: Should I Worry?

At 6 weeks, many women start feeling symptoms. But some don't, and that's OK.

## Table of Contents

1. [Why You Might Have No Symptoms at 6 Weeks](#s1)
2. [When to Check](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Why You Might Have No Symptoms at 6 Weeks {#s1}

- hCG may still be relatively low
- You may be less sensitive to hormones
- Your dates might be slightly off (you're earlier than 6 weeks)
- Some women just don't get symptoms early

## When to Check {#s2}

If you're worried, a private scan at 7-8 weeks can confirm the heartbeat. But no symptoms at 6 weeks alone isn't a reason to panic.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Should I be worried about no symptoms at 6 weeks?

No. While many women have symptoms at 6 weeks, many don't. If you're worried, a scan at 7-8 weeks can provide reassurance.
