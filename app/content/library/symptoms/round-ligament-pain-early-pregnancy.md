# Round Ligament Pain in Early Pregnancy

Round ligament pain is a sharp, stretching pain on one or both sides of your lower abdomen. It's one of the most common pregnancy pains.

## Table of Contents

1. [What It Feels Like](#s1)
2. [Relief](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## What It Feels Like {#s1}

A sharp, stabbing, or pulling sensation in your lower abdomen, usually on one side. It often happens when you change position, cough, sneeze, or stand up quickly.

## Relief {#s2}

Change positions slowly. Rest. Use a pregnancy support belt. Warm baths. Gentle stretching. Paracetamol if needed.

## FAQ {#faq}

### When does round ligament pain start?

It can start as early as 6-8 weeks but is more common from 10-12 weeks onwards as your uterus grows. It continues throughout pregnancy.
