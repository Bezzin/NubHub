# Pelvic Pain in Early Pregnancy

Pelvic pain is common in early pregnancy. Here's what's causing it and when to seek help.

## Table of Contents

1. [Causes](#s1)
2. [When to Worry](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Causes {#s1}

Round ligament pain (stretching ligaments), hormonal changes loosening joints, constipation and gas, and your expanding uterus. Most pelvic pain is normal.

## When to Worry {#s2}

Severe pain, one-sided pain (possible ectopic), pain with bleeding, or pain with fever needs urgent medical attention. Mild aching is usually fine.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is pelvic pain normal at 8 weeks?

Yes. Mild pelvic aching is normal as your uterus grows and ligaments stretch. Severe or one-sided pain should be checked urgently.
