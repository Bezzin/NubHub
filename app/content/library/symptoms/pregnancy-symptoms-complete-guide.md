# Pregnancy Symptoms Complete Guide

Complete guide to all first trimester pregnancy symptoms. What's normal, what's not, and when to get help.

## Overview

Your complete guide to every symptom you might experience in the first 12 weeks of pregnancy.

## FAQ

### First trimester symptoms include: missed period, nausea/morning sickness, fatigue, breast tenderness, frequent urination, heightened smell, food aversions and cravings, mood swings, bloating, constipation, heartburn, dizziness, headaches, acne, and mild cramping. Most are caused by hormonal changes and are completely normal.

What are the most common early pregnancy symptoms?

### The most common are: missed period (98%), nausea (80%), fatigue (80%), breast tenderness (76%), and frequent urination (60%). Most women experience at least 2-3 of these.
