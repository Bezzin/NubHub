## FAQ

### Week 4: missed period, possibly mild symptoms. Week 5: fatigue, breast tenderness. Week 6: morning sickness begins, frequent urination. Week 7: exhaustion peaks, food aversions. Week 8: bloating, constipation, heightened smell. Weeks 9-10: symptoms may be at their peak. Weeks 11-12: symptoms may start easing. Weeks 12-14: most symptoms ease for most women.

When do pregnancy symptoms usually start?
