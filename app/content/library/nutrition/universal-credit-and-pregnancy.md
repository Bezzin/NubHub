# Universal Credit and Pregnancy

Pregnancy can affect your Universal Credit claim. Here's what you need to know.

## What Changes

When you report your pregnancy to Universal Credit: you may become eligible for Healthy Start vouchers, you may be referred to a health visitor, and your work requirements may change (especially in the third trimester).

## After Birth

After your baby is born, your Universal Credit will increase. You'll get an additional amount for the child. You may also qualify for the Sure Start Maternity Grant.

## FAQ

### Should I tell Universal Credit I'm pregnant?

Yes. Report your pregnancy in your online account. This ensures you get all the support you're entitled to and may adjust your work requirements.

### Does Universal Credit increase when you have a baby?

Yes. You'll get an additional standard amount for each child. The exact amount depends on your circumstances. Report the birth as soon as possible.
