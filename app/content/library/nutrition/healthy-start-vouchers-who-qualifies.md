# Healthy Start Vouchers: Who Qualifies?

Healthy Start provides free food and milk vouchers to eligible pregnant women and families. Here's what you need to know.

## Who Qualifies

You qualify if you're at least 10 weeks pregnant and receive: Universal Credit (with a family income of £408 or less), Income Support, Income-based JSA, or Child Tax Credit (with income under £16,190). You also qualify if you're under 18.

## What You Get

- £4.25 per week per child (or pregnancy)
- Can be spent on: milk, fruit, vegetables, infant formula, and pulses
- Free vitamins for pregnant women and children

## How to Apply

Apply online at healthystart.nhs.uk or ask your midwife for a form.

## FAQ

### How much are Healthy Start vouchers worth?

£4.25 per week. If you're pregnant and have a child under 4, you get vouchers for both. They can be spent on milk, fruit, vegetables, and infant formula.

### When can I apply for Healthy Start?

From 10 weeks pregnant. Apply as soon as you qualify. You'll need your National Insurance number and proof of benefits.
