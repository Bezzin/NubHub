# Halal Pregnancy Diet Guide

Following a halal diet during pregnancy? Complete guide to nutrition and safety.

## Overview

Eating a halal diet during pregnancy is perfectly healthy. Here's what to keep in mind.

## FAQ

### A halal pregnancy diet provides all the nutrients you and your baby need. Ensure you get enough: iron (from halal red meat, lentils, spinach), calcium (from dairy or fortified alternatives), omega-3 (from fish or algae supplements), and folate (from leafy greens). Avoid raw/undercooked meats and ensure all food is halal-certified.

Is a halal diet safe in pregnancy?

### Yes. A halal diet is safe and nutritious during pregnancy. Ensure you eat a balanced diet with plenty of fruit, vegetables, protein, and calcium. Take folic acid and vitamin D supplements.
