# Kosher Pregnancy Diet Guide

Following a kosher diet during pregnancy? Complete guide to nutrition and safety.

## Overview

Eating a kosher diet during pregnancy is healthy and safe. Here's what to know.

## FAQ

### A kosher pregnancy diet provides all necessary nutrients. Ensure adequate: iron (from kosher meat, fish, lentils), calcium (from dairy), omega-3 (from fish), and folate. The kosher requirement to separate meat and dairy doesn't affect nutrition — just plan meals accordingly.

Is a kosher diet safe in pregnancy?

### Yes. A kosher diet is safe and nutritious during pregnancy. Focus on balanced meals with plenty of vegetables, protein, and calcium. Take folic acid and vitamin D.
