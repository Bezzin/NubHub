# Best Fish Oil for Pregnancy

Omega-3 fatty acids (DHA and EPA) are important for your baby's brain and eye development. Here's how to choose a safe fish oil.

## Table of Contents

1. [What to Look For](#s1)
2. [Benefits](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## What to Look For {#s1}

Choose a fish oil that: is molecularly distilled (removes mercury), has high DHA content, is from small fish (lower mercury), and is third-party tested. Algae-based DHA is a vegan alternative.

## Benefits {#s2}

Omega-3 supports: baby's brain development, eye development, and may reduce preterm birth risk. The NHS recommends eating 2 portions of fish per week, including one oily fish.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is fish oil safe in pregnancy?

Yes, high-quality fish oil supplements are safe in pregnancy. Avoid cod liver oil (contains vitamin A). Look for purified fish oil or algae-based DHA supplements.
