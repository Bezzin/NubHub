## Is Yoghurt Safe in Pregnancy? {#is-it-safe}

Yoghurt is a dairy that is safe to eat during pregnancy when properly prepared and stored.

The main concerns with food in pregnancy are listeria (a bacteria that can cause serious illness), toxoplasmosis (a parasite), salmonella (a bacteria), and vitamin A toxicity.

## NHS Guidance on Yoghurt {#nhs-guidance}

The NHS advises that yes - all yoghurt is safe in pregnancy. Always follow NHS food safety guidance during pregnancy.

## How to Eat Yoghurt Safely {#how-to-eat-safely}

Yoghurt is safe to eat during pregnancy. Follow general food hygiene: check use-by dates, store at correct temperature, and prepare cleanly.

## Frequently Asked Questions {#faq}

### What if I accidentally ate yoghurt while pregnant?

Don't panic. The risk of infection is low. Watch for symptoms like fever, nausea, vomiting, or diarrhoea. If you feel unwell, contact your GP or call 111.

### Is yoghurt safe in the first trimester?

The same rules apply throughout pregnancy. Yes - all yoghurt is safe in pregnancy
