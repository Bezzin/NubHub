# Sure Start Maternity Grant: Who Qualifies?

The Sure Start Maternity Grant is a £500 one-off payment to help with the costs of a new baby.

## Who Qualifies

You qualify if you receive: Universal Credit, Income Support, Income-based JSA, Child Tax Credit, or Pension Credit, AND this is your first child (or you have a multiple birth).

## How to Claim

Claim using form SF100. You can claim from 29 weeks pregnant until your baby is 6 months old. The grant doesn't need to be repaid.

## FAQ

### How much is the Sure Start Maternity Grant?

£500. It's a one-off payment that doesn't need to be repaid. You can use it for anything baby-related.

### Can I get the Sure Start Maternity Grant for my second baby?

Only if it's a multiple birth (twins, triplets). For single babies, it's only available for your first child.
