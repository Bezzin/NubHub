# Pregnancy and Relationship Stress

Pregnancy can strain relationships. Here's how to manage stress together.

## Table of Contents

1. [Why Stress Increases](#s1)
2. [Managing Together](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Why Stress Increases {#s1}

Financial worries, changing roles, hormone-driven mood swings, fear about the future, and physical exhaustion all contribute to relationship stress in pregnancy.

## Managing Together {#s2}

Communicate openly. Share your fears. Make decisions together. Give each other grace. Consider counselling if needed. Remember: this is a phase, not forever.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is relationship stress normal in pregnancy?

Yes. Pregnancy brings major life changes that stress relationships. Open communication, shared decision-making, and professional support if needed can help you navigate this together.
