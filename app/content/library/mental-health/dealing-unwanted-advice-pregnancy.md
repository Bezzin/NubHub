# Dealing With Unwanted Advice in Pregnancy

Everyone has opinions about pregnancy. Here's how to handle unwanted advice gracefully.

## Table of Contents

1. [Why People Give Advice](#s1)
2. [How to Respond](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Why People Give Advice {#s1}

Most people mean well — they're excited and want to share their experience. But it can feel overwhelming and critical.

## How to Respond {#s2}

- 'Thanks, I'll keep that in mind' (then ignore it)
- 'My midwife has advised me on that'
- 'We've decided to do X'
- 'I'm comfortable with my choices'
- Change the subject
- Set boundaries with persistent advice-givers

## FAQ {#faq}

### How do I stop unwanted pregnancy advice?

Use polite deflections like 'Thanks, I'll keep that in mind' or 'My midwife has advised me on that.' For persistent advice-givers, be direct: 'I appreciate your concern, but I'm following my healthcare team's guidance.'
