# Making a Wellbeing Plan in Pregnancy

A wellbeing plan helps you think about how you feel and what support you might need. Here's how to make one.

## Table of Contents

1. [What Is a Wellbeing Plan?](#s1)
2. [How to Make One](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## What Is a Wellbeing Plan? {#s1}

A wellbeing plan is a document that helps you identify your mental health needs during pregnancy and after birth. It covers: your emotional wellbeing, support networks, warning signs to watch for, and what to do if you're struggling.

## How to Make One {#s2}

Tommy's offers a free wellbeing plan template. Complete it with your midwife. Include: your support network, things that help you cope, signs that you need extra support, and who to contact in crisis.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Why should I make a wellbeing plan?

A wellbeing plan helps you and your healthcare team identify mental health needs early. It ensures you get support proactively rather than waiting until you're in crisis. It's recommended by the NHS and Tommy's.
