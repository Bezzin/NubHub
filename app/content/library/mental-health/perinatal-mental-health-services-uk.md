# Perinatal Mental Health Services in the UK: What's Available

The UK has specialist perinatal mental health services. Here's what's available and how to access them.

## Table of Contents

1. [What's Available](#section-1)
2. [How to Access Support](#section-2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## What's Available {#section-1}

- **Perinatal mental health teams**: Specialist teams in every NHS region
- **GP**: First point of contact for mental health concerns
- **Midwife**: Can refer you to specialist services
- **Health visitor**: Supports mental health after birth
- **Talking therapies**: NHS talking therapies (IAPT)
- **Charities**: PANDAS, Tommy's, Mind, Maternal Mental Health Alliance

## How to Access Support {#section-2}

1. Tell your GP or midwife how you're feeling
2. They can refer you to perinatal mental health services
3. You may be offered therapy, medication, or both
4. Support is free on the NHS
5. You can also self-refer to IAPT in many areas

## FAQ {#faq}

### How do I access perinatal mental health services?

Tell your GP or midwife how you're feeling. They can refer you to specialist perinatal mental health services. You can also self-refer to NHS talking therapies (IAPT) in many areas.

### Is perinatal mental health support free?

Yes. All NHS perinatal mental health services are free. This includes GP appointments, midwife support, specialist teams, talking therapies, and medication if needed.
