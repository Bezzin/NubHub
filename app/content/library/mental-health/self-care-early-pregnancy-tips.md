# Self-Care in Early Pregnancy: Practical Tips

Self-care in early pregnancy isn't a luxury — it's essential. Here are practical tips for looking after yourself.

## Table of Contents

1. [Physical Self-Care](#s1)
2. [Emotional Self-Care](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Physical Self-Care {#s1}

Rest when tired. Eat nourishing food. Stay hydrated. Take your vitamins. Get gentle exercise. Prioritise sleep. Wear comfortable clothes.

## Emotional Self-Care {#s2}

Be kind to yourself. Accept that you can't do everything. Set boundaries. Limit stress. Talk about your feelings. Take breaks from pregnancy forums. Do things you enjoy.

## FAQ {#faq}

### How do I practice self-care in early pregnancy?

Prioritise rest, eat well, take your vitamins, be kind to yourself, set boundaries, and do things you enjoy. Self-care isn't selfish — it's essential for you and your baby.
