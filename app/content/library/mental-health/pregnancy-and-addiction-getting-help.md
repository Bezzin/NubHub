# Pregnancy And Addiction Getting Help

Struggling with addiction during pregnancy? Where to get help without judgment.

## Overview

If you're struggling with addiction during pregnancy, help is available without judgment. Here's where to turn.

## FAQ

### Your GP and midwife are there to help, not judge. They can refer you to specialist services. Other support: FRANK (drug information, 0300 123 6600), NHS smoking cessation services, and local addiction services. Be honest with your healthcare team — they need to know to provide the best care for you and your baby.

Will I get in trouble for substance use in pregnancy?

### No. Healthcare professionals are there to help, not punish. They need to know about any substance use to provide safe care. Be honest — it's the best thing for your baby.
