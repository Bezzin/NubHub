# Journaling in Pregnancy: How It Helps

Keeping a pregnancy journal can be a powerful tool for mental health and memory-keeping. Here's how it helps.

## Table of Contents

1. [Benefits](#s1)
2. [How to Start](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Benefits {#s1}

Journaling can: reduce anxiety, process complex emotions, track symptoms, create a keepsake for your child, identify patterns, and provide a safe space for difficult feelings.

## How to Start {#s2}

Write for 5-10 minutes daily. No rules — just write what you feel. Track symptoms, appointments, and milestones. Write letters to your baby. Include scan photos.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Should I journal during pregnancy?

Yes. Journaling reduces anxiety, creates a keepsake, and helps process emotions. Even 5 minutes a day can make a difference. There's no right or wrong way to do it.
