# 4 Weeks Pregnant: Your FAQs Answered

At 4 weeks pregnant, common questions include: Is it too early to test? When should I see a GP? Can I exercise? What foods should I avoid? Is spotting normal?

## Overview

At 4 weeks, many women don't know they're pregnant yet. A test may be positive but could be faint. Start folic acid. Book a GP appointment. Avoid alcohol and unsafe foods.

## FAQ

### Is it too early to take a pregnancy test at 4 weeks?

At 4 weeks (which is when your period is due), a pregnancy test should be positive. Some sensitive tests can detect pregnancy a few days before your missed period.

### Should I have any symptoms at 4 weeks?

Some women have symptoms (fatigue, breast tenderness) but many have none at all. Both are normal at 4 weeks.

### What should I do?

Always consult your healthcare provider for personalised advice.
