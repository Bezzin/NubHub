# 6 Weeks Pregnant: What to Do This Week

At 6 weeks, attend your booking appointment if scheduled. Start a pregnancy journal. Manage morning sickness with small meals.

## Checklist {#s1}

At 6 weeks, attend your booking appointment if scheduled. Start a pregnancy journal. Manage morning sickness with small meals.

- Take folic acid daily
- Avoid alcohol and unsafe foods
- Stay hydrated
- Rest when needed

## Tips {#s2}

- Listen to your body
- Don't push yourself too hard
- Start planning for your next appointment
- Enjoy this special time

## FAQ {#faq}

### What should I prioritise at 6 weeks?

At 6 weeks, attend your booking appointment if scheduled. Start a pregnancy journal. Manage morning sickness with small meals.

### When should I tell people I'm pregnant?

Most women wait until after their 12-week scan to announce publicly, but telling close family earlier is common.
