# 8 Weeks Pregnant: Baby Development

At 8 weeks pregnant, your baby is approximately the size of a kidney bean. Fingers and toes begin to form. Facial features become more defined and organs develop.

## Baby Size at 8 Weeks {#baby-size}

Your baby is about the size of a kidney bean. Growth is rapid during the first trimester.

## Development Milestones {#milestones}

Key developments at 8 weeks:
- Fingers and toes begin to form
- Facial features become more defined and organs develop
- Your baby is growing rapidly
- The heart is beating

## FAQ {#faq}

### How big is my baby at 8 weeks?

Your baby is approximately the size of a kidney bean.

### Can I see my baby on a scan at 8 weeks?

Yes, a scan at 8 weeks should show your baby and possibly the heartbeat.
