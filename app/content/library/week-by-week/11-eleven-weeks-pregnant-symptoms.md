# 11 Weeks Pregnant: Your Symptoms

At 11 weeks pregnant you're getting ready for your 12-week scan. Symptoms may include nausea easing, increased appetite, linea nigra appearing, and energy slowly returning. This is a good time to prepare for your scan.

## Common Symptoms at 11 Weeks {#common-symptoms}

At 11 weeks, you may experience some or none of these symptoms. Every pregnancy is different.

- Fatigue and tiredness
- Nausea and morning sickness
- Breast tenderness
- Frequent urination
- Mood swings
- Bloating
- Food cravings or aversions
- Heightened sense of smell

## What's Normal at 11 Weeks {#whats-normal}

It's completely normal to have some, all, or none of these symptoms. Having no symptoms doesn't mean something is wrong. Symptoms come and go throughout early pregnancy as hormone levels fluctuate.

## When to Worry {#when-to-worry}

Contact your GP or midwife if you experience:
- Heavy bleeding (soaking a pad)
- Severe abdominal pain
- Fever above 38C
- Inability to keep fluids down for 24 hours
- Sudden loss of all pregnancy symptoms (especially after seeing a heartbeat)

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is it normal to have no symptoms at 11 weeks?

Yes. Many women have no symptoms in early pregnancy. This doesn't mean anything is wrong.

### When will my symptoms ease?

Most women find symptoms ease between weeks 12-14 as the first trimester ends.

### Should I be worried about cramping at 11 weeks?

Mild cramping is normal. Severe pain or cramping with heavy bleeding needs urgent medical attention.
