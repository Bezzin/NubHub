# 10 Weeks Pregnant: Baby Development

At 10 weeks pregnant, your baby is approximately the size of a strawberry. Bones start to harden and nails begin forming. The baby can move although you can't feel it yet.

## Baby Size at 10 Weeks {#baby-size}

Your baby is about the size of a strawberry. Growth is rapid during the first trimester.

## Development Milestones {#milestones}

Key developments at 10 weeks:
- Bones start to harden and nails begin forming
- The baby can move although you can't feel it yet
- Your baby is growing rapidly
- The heart is beating

## FAQ {#faq}

### How big is my baby at 10 weeks?

Your baby is approximately the size of a strawberry.

### Can I see my baby on a scan at 10 weeks?

Yes, a scan at 10 weeks should show your baby and possibly the heartbeat.
