# 5 Weeks Pregnant: Your FAQs Answered

At 5 weeks, common questions include: When do I see a midwife? Is cramping normal? What if I have no symptoms? Can I still exercise?

## Overview

Contact your GP to book your first midwife appointment. Start folic acid. Mild cramping and breast tenderness are normal. You can continue exercise.

## FAQ

### When should I book my first midwife appointment?

Contact your GP as soon as you have a positive test. They'll book your 'booking appointment' for around 8-10 weeks.

### Is cramping normal at 5 weeks?

Mild cramping is normal — your uterus is starting to grow. Severe pain or cramping with bleeding needs medical attention.

### What should I do?

Always consult your healthcare provider for personalised advice.
