# Best Pregnancy Podcasts UK

Podcasts are a flexible way to absorb pregnancy information while commuting, walking, or nesting. Whether you want reassuring birth stories, expert interviews, or week-by-week updates, here are some of the best pregnancy podcasts for UK listeners.

## UK pregnancy podcasts {uk-podcasts}

The Midwives' Cauldron is a UK-based podcast from midwives covering evidence and birth culture. The Birth Hour features birth stories from women in the UK and beyond, useful for hearing varied experiences. The Parenting Podcast from Parenting Hell creators offers a humorous, relatable take on family life, including pregnancy. These podcasts are free on Spotify and Apple Podcasts.

## Birth stories and positivity {birth-stories}

The Birth Hour is dedicated to birth stories in many forms: home, hospital, caesarean, VBAC, and more. Listening to positive, varied stories can reduce fear and prepare you for different outcomes. The Birthful podcast with Adriana Lozada mixes stories with expert interviews on labour, feeding, and postnatal care.

## Expert-led podcasts {expert-led}

Pregnancy, Birth and Beyond from the Royal College of Midwives offers evidence-based episodes from UK midwives. The Evidence Based Birth podcast, while US-focused, covers topics relevant to UK parents making informed choices. Dr Midwife and The Midwives' Cauldron feature qualified professionals discussing current practice and research.

## Choosing the right podcast {choosing}

If you want reassurance, start with birth story podcasts. If you want facts, try expert-led shows. If you want company and humour, look for parenting duos. Limit your listening if podcasts make you anxious; not every story will match your experience. Curate a playlist that informs and uplifts rather than overwhelms.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Are pregnancy podcasts free?

Most are free with ads on Spotify and Apple Podcasts. Some offer ad-free paid tiers or bonus episodes.

### Can podcasts replace antenatal classes?

They complement but don't replace classes. Use them for background learning and reassurance alongside structured courses.

### Which podcast is best for first-time parents?

The Birth Hour and The Midwives' Cauldron are widely recommended for accessible, balanced content.
