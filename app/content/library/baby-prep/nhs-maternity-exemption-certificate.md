# NHS Maternity Exemption Certificate: How to Apply

Your maternity exemption certificate (MatEx) gives you free NHS prescriptions and dental care during pregnancy and for 12 months after birth.

## Table of Contents

1. [How to Apply](#s1)
2. [What It Covers](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## How to Apply {#s1}

1. Ask your GP, midwife, or health visitor for a FW8 form
2. Fill in your details
3. Your midwife or GP signs it
4. Send it to the NHS Business Services Authority
5. You'll receive your MatEx card in 2-3 weeks

## What It Covers {#s2}

- Free NHS prescriptions
- Free NHS dental treatment
- Valid from the date your form is signed until 12 months after your due date

## FAQ {#faq}

### When should I apply for my maternity exemption certificate?

Apply as soon as you have your booking appointment. Your midwife can sign the form. You'll get free prescriptions and dental care throughout pregnancy and for 12 months after birth.
