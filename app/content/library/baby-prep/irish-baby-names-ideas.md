# Irish Baby Names: Beautiful Ideas

Irish baby names are popular in the UK for their beautiful meanings and spellings. Here are some ideas.

## Table of Contents

1. [Popular Irish Girl Names](#s1)
2. [Popular Irish Boy Names](#s2)

## Popular Irish Girl Names {#s1}

Aoife (beautiful), Niamh (radiance), Saoirse (freedom), Ciara (dark-haired), Maeve (intoxicating), Erin (Ireland), Orla (golden princess), Roisin (little rose)

## Popular Irish Boy Names {#s2}

Cillian (church), Conor (lover of wolves), Declan (man of prayer), Finn (fair), Liam (strong-willed), Ronan (little seal), Senan (old), Tadhg (poet)

## FAQ {#faq}

### Are Irish names popular in the UK?

Yes. Irish names like Conor, Niamh, and Saoirse are increasingly popular in the UK. They offer beautiful meanings and a connection to Irish heritage.
