# NCT Classes: What They Cover

NCT (National Childbirth Trust) classes are among the most popular private antenatal courses in the UK. They are known for small group sizes, comprehensive content, and the lifelong friendships many parents make. Here is what NCT classes cover and how to decide if they are right for you.

## What NCT classes cover {what-they-cover}

NCT Signature classes typically run over 15-20 hours across several weeks or a weekend. Topics include what happens during labour, pain relief options, birth positions, medical interventions, feeding (breast and bottle), early parenthood, and postnatal mental health. Classes include practical activities, discussions, and relaxation techniques. Your partner is encouraged to attend.

## The social side {social-side}

One of the biggest draws of NCT is meeting other parents due around the same time in your area. Many groups stay in touch for years, sharing advice, playdates, and support. This social network is often cited as worth the fee alone, especially for first-time parents who may not have local parent friends.

## Costs and concessions {costs}

NCT Signature classes cost around 200 to 300 pounds per couple, though prices vary by location and group size. NCT offers discounts and concessions for those on lower incomes, under-25s, and single parents. Some areas offer shorter, lower-cost NCT Essentials classes. Check the NCT website for pricing in your area and available bursaries.

## Are NCT classes worth it? {worth-it}

If you value small-group learning, partner involvement, and building a local parent network, NCT is highly rated. If you are confident about birth, prefer self-directed learning, or have budget constraints, NHS classes may suffice. Many parents combine NHS classes with NCT for the best of both. Read reviews and talk to recent attendees before deciding.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Do NCT classes teach hypnobirthing?

Some NCT teachers include relaxation techniques, but dedicated hypnobirthing is usually a separate course. Ask your local NCT teacher what is included.

### Can I attend NCT classes alone?

Yes, solo parents are welcome. Let the organiser know in advance so they can support you.

### What if I can't afford NCT classes?

NCT offers concessions and bursaries. You can also access free NHS classes or local parent groups.
