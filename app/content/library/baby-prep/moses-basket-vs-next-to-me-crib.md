# Moses Basket vs Next-to-Me Crib: Which Is Better?

Choosing where your newborn will sleep is a big decision. Here's how moses baskets and next-to-me cribs compare.

## Moses Basket

Pros: lightweight, portable, cheaper, traditional. Cons: baby outgrows it by 3-4 months, less sturdy.

## Next-to-Me Crib

Pros: baby sleeps next to you (safer for night feeds), lasts longer (5-6 months), one side drops down for easy access, some have rocking functions. Cons: more expensive, less portable.

## FAQ

### Is a next-to-me crib better than a moses basket?

For most parents, yes. The side-zip access makes night feeds much easier, and they last longer. However, moses baskets are cheaper and more portable. Both are safe for newborns.

### How long does a baby use a moses basket?

Usually 3-4 months, or until your baby can roll or push up. Next-to-me cribs last longer — typically 5-6 months.
