# Decorating a Nursery: When to Start

Wondering when to start on the nursery? Here's our guide.

## Table of Contents

1. [When to Start](#s1)
2. [Safety Considerations](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## When to Start {#s1}

Most parents start in the second trimester (weeks 14-27) when energy returns. The nursery should be finished by 36 weeks in case the baby comes early.

## Safety Considerations {#s2}

Use pregnancy-safe paint (water-based, low VOC). Ensure good ventilation. Don't paint yourself if the smell bothers you. Avoid heavy lifting while decorating.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is it safe to paint while pregnant?

Water-based (latex) paints with low VOCs are generally safe in pregnancy. Avoid oil-based paints and solvent-based products. Ensure good ventilation and take breaks. Better yet, get someone else to paint!
