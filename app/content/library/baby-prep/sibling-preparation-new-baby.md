# Sibling Preparation New Baby

How to prepare older children for a new sibling. Age-appropriate tips and advice.

## Overview

Preparing older children for a new baby helps reduce jealousy and anxiety. Here's how.

## FAQ

### For toddlers: read books about new siblings, show them scan photos, involve them in choosing baby items, and reassure them they're loved. For older children: explain what will happen when the baby arrives, discuss their role as a big sibling, and involve them in preparation. All ages: maintain routines and give extra attention.

When should I tell my toddler about the new baby?

### Wait until you're showing or past the first trimester (around 12-16 weeks). Toddlers don't understand long timeframes — telling them at 6 weeks means they'll expect a baby tomorrow.
