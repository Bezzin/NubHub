# How to Choose a Baby Name: Complete Guide

Choosing a baby name is a big decision. Here's a complete guide to help you decide.

## Table of Contents

1. [Tips for Choosing](#s1)
2. [Common Mistakes](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Tips for Choosing {#s1}

- Say the full name out loud with your surname
- Check initials don't spell anything unfortunate
- Consider nicknames and short forms
- Think about how the name ages (will it suit an adult?)
- Check popularity trends (do you want a common or unique name?)
- Consider family traditions
- Make sure both parents agree

## Common Mistakes {#s2}

- Choosing a name that's too trendy (may date quickly)
- Not considering teasing potential
- Forgetting to check initials
- Letting family pressure override your preference

## FAQ {#faq}

### When should I start thinking about baby names?

You can start anytime, but many parents begin in the second trimester. Don't feel rushed — you have until birth to decide. Some parents even wait until they meet their baby.
