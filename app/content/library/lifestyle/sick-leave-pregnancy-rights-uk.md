# Sick Leave in Pregnancy: Your Rights in the UK

If you're too ill to work during pregnancy, you have the right to take sick leave. Here's what you need to know.

## Your Rights

You can take sick leave for pregnancy-related illness without it counting against your normal sick leave entitlement. Your employer cannot force you to start maternity leave early if you're off sick before 36 weeks.

## Sick Pay

You're entitled to Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) at £109.40/week for up to 28 weeks, or your company's sick pay scheme if it offers more.

## FAQ

### Can pregnancy sickness count as sick leave?

Yes. If morning sickness or any pregnancy-related illness prevents you from working, you can take sick leave. Your employer should not penalise you for pregnancy-related absence.

### Can my employer start my maternity leave if I'm off sick?

Only if you're off sick in the last 4 weeks before your due date. Before that, you can take sick leave without it triggering maternity leave.
