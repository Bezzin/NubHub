# Exercises to Avoid in Pregnancy

Some exercises are unsafe during pregnancy. Here's what to avoid and why.

## Table of Contents

1. [Exercises to Avoid](#s1)
2. [Safe Alternatives](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Exercises to Avoid {#s1}

- Contact sports (rugby, martial arts)
- Scuba diving (pressure changes)
- Hot yoga (overheating risk)
- Exercises on your back (after 16 weeks)
- High-impact with fall risk (skiing, gymnastics)
- Heavy lifting beyond your capacity
- Altitude training above 2,500m

## Safe Alternatives {#s2}

Walking, swimming, pregnancy yoga, Pilates, stationary cycling, and low-impact aerobics are all safe.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Can I do HIIT while pregnant?

If you did HIIT before pregnancy, you can continue at reduced intensity. Avoid overheating and high-impact moves. If you're new to exercise, start gentler.
