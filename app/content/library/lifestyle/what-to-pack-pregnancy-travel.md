# What to Pack for Pregnancy Travel

Travelling while pregnant requires some extra planning. Here's what to pack.

## Table of Contents

1. [Essentials](#s1)
2. [Tips](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Essentials {#s1}

- Your maternity notes
- Any medications you're taking
- Prenatal vitamins
- Anti-sickness remedies (ginger sweets, acupressure bands)
- Compression socks for long journeys
- Snacks for nausea
- Water bottle
- Pregnancy-safe sunscreen
- Comfortable shoes

## FAQ {#faq}

### Should I take my maternity notes when travelling?

Yes, always take your maternity notes when travelling. If you need medical care while away, they provide essential information about your pregnancy.
