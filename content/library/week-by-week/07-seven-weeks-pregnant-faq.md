# 7 Weeks Pregnant: Your FAQs Answered

At 7 weeks, common questions: Is extreme tiredness normal? What if I can't eat? When do symptoms peak? Can I take paracetamol?

## Overview

Extreme tiredness peaks around 7-8 weeks. Food aversions are common. Paracetamol is safe. Symptoms vary day to day — this is normal.

## FAQ

### Is extreme tiredness normal at 7 weeks?

Yes. At 7 weeks, fatigue is often at its worst. Your body is making the placenta and hormones are surging. Rest as much as possible.

### Can I take paracetamol at 7 weeks?

Yes, paracetamol is considered safe in pregnancy at the recommended dose. Avoid ibuprofen.

### What should I do?

Always consult your healthcare provider for personalised advice.
