# 6 Weeks Pregnant: Baby Development

At 6 weeks pregnant, your baby is approximately the size of a lentil. The heart starts beating and can be seen on ultrasound. Limb buds appear and facial features begin forming.

## Baby Size at 6 Weeks {#baby-size}

Your baby is about the size of a lentil. Growth is rapid during the first trimester.

## Development Milestones {#milestones}

Key developments at 6 weeks:
- The heart starts beating and can be seen on ultrasound
- Limb buds appear and facial features begin forming
- Your baby is growing rapidly
- The heart is beating

## FAQ {#faq}

### How big is my baby at 6 weeks?

Your baby is approximately the size of a lentil.

### Can I see my baby on a scan at 6 weeks?

Yes, a scan at 6 weeks should show your baby and possibly the heartbeat.
