# 4 Weeks Pregnant: Baby Development

At 4 weeks pregnant, your baby is approximately the size of a poppy seed. The embryo implants in the uterus lining. The neural tube begins to form.

## Baby Size at 4 Weeks {#baby-size}

Your baby is about the size of a poppy seed. Growth is rapid during the first trimester.

## Development Milestones {#milestones}

Key developments at 4 weeks:
- The embryo implants in the uterus lining
- The neural tube begins to form
- Your baby is growing rapidly
- The heart is beating

## FAQ {#faq}

### How big is my baby at 4 weeks?

Your baby is approximately the size of a poppy seed.

### Can I see my baby on a scan at 4 weeks?

At 4 weeks it may be too early for a scan to show much. Most scans are done from 6+ weeks.
