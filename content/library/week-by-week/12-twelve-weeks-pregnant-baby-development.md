# 12 Weeks Pregnant: Baby Development

At 12 weeks pregnant, your baby is approximately the size of a plum. Reflexes develop and facial features are distinct. Sex organs are forming - ideal time for nub theory.

## Baby Size at 12 Weeks {#baby-size}

Your baby is about the size of a plum. Growth is rapid during the first trimester.

## Development Milestones {#milestones}

Key developments at 12 weeks:
- Reflexes develop and facial features are distinct
- Sex organs are forming - ideal time for nub theory
- Your baby is growing rapidly
- The heart is beating

## FAQ {#faq}

### How big is my baby at 12 weeks?

Your baby is approximately the size of a plum.

### Can I see my baby on a scan at 12 weeks?

Yes, your 12-week scan around this time will give you a clear view of your baby.
