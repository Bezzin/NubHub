# Red Flag Symptoms Early Pregnancy Complete

Complete guide to red flag symptoms in early pregnancy — when to call 999, go to A&E, or call 111.

## Overview

Knowing the red flag symptoms in early pregnancy can save your life. Here's what to watch for.

## FAQ

### Call 999: severe bleeding (soaking a pad in an hour), collapse, severe sudden pain. Go to A&E: heavy bleeding, severe abdominal pain, shoulder pain with bleeding, fever above 38C. Call 111: light bleeding, mild cramping, persistent vomiting, signs of UTI.

What are red flag symptoms in early pregnancy?

### Red flags: heavy bleeding (soaking a pad in an hour), severe abdominal pain, shoulder tip pain, collapse or fainting, fever above 38C, and inability to keep fluids down for 24 hours. Seek urgent medical attention.
