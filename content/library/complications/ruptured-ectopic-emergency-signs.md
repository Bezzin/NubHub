# Ruptured Ectopic Emergency Signs

A ruptured ectopic pregnancy is a life-threatening emergency. Know the signs.

## Overview

A ruptured ectopic pregnancy is a medical emergency. Here are the signs.

## FAQ

### Signs of a ruptured ectopic: severe, sudden abdominal pain, shoulder tip pain, dizziness or collapse, rapid heartbeat, paleness, and feeling faint. This is a life-threatening emergency — call 999 immediately. The fallopian tube has burst and is causing internal bleeding. Surgery is needed urgently.
