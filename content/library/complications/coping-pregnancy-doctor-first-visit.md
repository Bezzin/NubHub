# Coping Pregnancy Doctor First Visit

Your first GP visit in pregnancy — what to expect, what they'll check, and questions to ask.

## Overview

Your first GP visit in pregnancy is an important step. Here's what to expect.

## FAQ

### At your first GP visit: they'll confirm your pregnancy, calculate your due date, refer you to midwifery services, check your blood pressure and weight, review medications, discuss lifestyle (smoking, alcohol, diet), and arrange blood tests. Bring: a list of medications, your medical history, and questions.

What happens at the first GP appointment when pregnant?

### Your GP confirms the pregnancy, calculates your due date from your last period, refers you to a midwife, checks blood pressure and weight, reviews medications, and discusses lifestyle. It usually takes 15-20 minutes.
