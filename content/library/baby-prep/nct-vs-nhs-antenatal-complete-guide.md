## FAQ

### NCT: paid (£100-£300+), small groups (6-8 couples), makes lifelong friends, comprehensive content, runs over several weeks. NHS: free, larger groups, covers the basics, usually 1-2 sessions, run by midwives. Many parents do both — NCT for the social aspect and NHS for the local hospital information.

Are NCT classes worth the money?

### For many parents, yes. The main value is the friendship group — NCT friends often become your support network for years. The content is also comprehensive. However, if cost is an issue, NHS classes cover the essentials for free.
