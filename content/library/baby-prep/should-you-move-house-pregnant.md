# Should You Move House When Pregnant?

Moving house during pregnancy is possible but stressful. Here's what to consider.

## Table of Contents

1. [Pros](#s1)
2. [Cons](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Pros {#s1}

More space for the baby, closer to family support, better area for raising children, garden for when they're older.

## Cons {#s2}

Stress of moving, packing while tired, changing GP/midwife, registering with new hospital, disruption to antenatal care.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is it safe to move house during pregnancy?

Yes, it's safe but stressful. If you move, make sure to: register with a new GP immediately, transfer your maternity notes, and don't lift heavy boxes. Get help from friends and family.
