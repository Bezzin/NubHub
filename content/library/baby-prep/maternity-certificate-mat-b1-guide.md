# Maternity Certificate (Mat B1): Complete Guide

The Mat B1 (MATB1) is the certificate your midwife gives you to claim maternity pay. Here's what you need to know.

## Table of Contents

1. [What Is a Mat B1?](#s1)
2. [When You Get It](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## What Is a Mat B1? {#s1}

The Mat B1 is a certificate signed by your midwife or GP that confirms your expected date of delivery. Your employer needs it to process your Statutory Maternity Pay (SMP).

## When You Get It {#s2}

Your midwife will give you the Mat B1 at your 20-week appointment. You then give it to your employer at least 15 weeks before your due date.

## FAQ {#faq}

### When do I get my Mat B1?

Your midwife gives you the Mat B1 at your 20-week scan appointment. You need to give it to your employer to claim Statutory Maternity Pay.
