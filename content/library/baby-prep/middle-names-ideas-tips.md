# Middle Names: Ideas and Tips

Middle names give you a chance to add meaning, honour family, or just add something beautiful.

## Table of Contents

1. [Tips for Choosing](#s1)
2. [Popular Middle Names](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Tips for Choosing {#s1}

- Use a family name
- Choose something that flows well with the first and last name
- Consider a name that balances a modern first name with a traditional middle
- Use the middle name for something adventurous you wouldn't dare use as a first name
- Check the initials with the middle name included

## Popular Middle Names {#s2}

Grace, Rose, Louise, May, James, Thomas, Alexander, William, George

## FAQ {#faq}

### Do babies need a middle name?

No, a middle name is optional. But many parents use it to honour family, add meaning, or give their child options. Some people go by their middle name later in life.
