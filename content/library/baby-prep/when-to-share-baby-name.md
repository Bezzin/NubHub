# When to Share Your Baby Name

Deciding when to share your baby name is a personal choice. Here are the pros and cons.

## Table of Contents

1. [Sharing Early — Pros](#s1)
2. [Keeping It Secret — Pros](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Sharing Early — Pros {#s1}

- Get feedback on how the name sounds
- Check for negative associations
- Family can get used to it
- You can test initials and nicknames

## Keeping It Secret — Pros {#s2}

- Avoid unwanted opinions and criticism
- Keep some excitement for the birth
- Prevent name 'stealing' by friends
- Your choice, not a committee decision

## FAQ {#faq}

### Should I tell people my baby name before birth?

It's personal. If you share, be prepared for opinions. If you keep it secret, you avoid criticism but lose the chance to test reactions. Many parents share with close family only.
