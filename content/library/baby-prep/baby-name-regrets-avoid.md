# Baby Name Regrets: How to Avoid Them

Some parents regret their baby name choice. Here's how to avoid common pitfalls.

## Table of Contents

1. [Common Regrets](#s1)
2. [Avoiding Regrets](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Common Regrets {#s1}

- The name became too popular
- The name is constantly misspelled or mispronounced
- The nickname is undesirable
- A relative has the same name
- The name doesn't suit the child

## Avoiding Regrets {#s2}

- Test the name with friends and family
- Consider how it sounds with your surname
- Think about all possible nicknames
- Imagine your child at different ages with the name
- Take your time — don't decide under pressure

## FAQ {#faq}

### How common are baby name regrets?

Surveys suggest about 10-20% of parents have some regret about their baby name choice. Taking time to test the name and considering all angles can help avoid this.
