# What to Put on Your Baby Registry: Complete List

Here's a complete checklist for your baby registry.

## Essentials

- Car seat (new only)
- Pram or pushchair
- Crib or next-to-me + new mattress
- Nappies (newborn and size 1)
- Baby clothes (vests, sleepsuits, cardigans)
- Muslins (lots!)
- Baby monitor
- Changing mat
- Baby bath or bath support
- Feeding equipment (bottles, breast pump if needed)

## Nice to Have

- Baby swing or bouncer
- Playmat
- Baby carrier or sling
- Nursing pillow
- Sleep bag
- Bath thermometer

## Skip

- Bottle warmers (hot water works)
- Wipe warmers
- Nappy disposal systems (a bin works)
- Too many newborn clothes (they grow fast)

## FAQ

### How many baby clothes do I need?

About 7-10 vests and 7-10 sleepsuits for the newborn stage. Babies go through several outfit changes a day. Don't buy too many newborn size — move to 0-1 month quickly.

### What's the most useful baby gift?

Muslins, sleepsuits, and nappies are the most practical. Gift cards are also appreciated — they let parents buy what they need when they need it.
