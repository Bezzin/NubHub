# Pregnancy Finances: Getting Organised Early

Getting your finances in order during pregnancy reduces stress. Here's what to sort out in the first trimester.

## Table of Contents

1. [What to Sort Early](#s1)
2. [Budgeting](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## What to Sort Early {#s1}

Check your maternity pay entitlement (SMP or Maternity Allowance). Apply for a maternity exemption certificate (free prescriptions and dental). Check if you qualify for Healthy Start vouchers, Sure Start Maternity Grant, or Universal Credit.

## Budgeting {#s2}

Start a baby budget: list your income, current expenses, and estimated baby costs. Start saving if possible. Buy items gradually rather than all at once.

## FAQ {#faq}

### What financial help can I get when pregnant?

You may be entitled to Statutory Maternity Pay or Maternity Allowance, free prescriptions, free dental care, Healthy Start vouchers, Sure Start Maternity Grant (£500), and Universal Credit. Check with Citizens Advice.
