# Scottish Baby Names: Beautiful Ideas

Scottish baby names have deep roots and strong meanings. Here are some ideas.

## Table of Contents

1. [Popular Scottish Girl Names](#s1)
2. [Popular Scottish Boy Names](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Popular Scottish Girl Names {#s1}

Isla (island), Maisie (pearl), Skye (island), Bonnie (beautiful), Kirsty (follower of Christ), Ailsa (fairy rock), Elspeth (God is my oath)

## Popular Scottish Boy Names {#s2}

Callum (dove), Hamish (supplanter), Finlay (fair warrior), Lachlan (from the fjords), Ruaraidh (red king), Alasdair (defender of men)

## FAQ {#faq}

### Are Scottish names popular in the UK?

Yes. Names like Isla, Callum, and Maisie are popular across the UK. Scottish names often have strong meanings and connections to nature and heritage.
