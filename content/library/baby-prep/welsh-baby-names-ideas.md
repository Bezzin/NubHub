# Welsh Baby Names: Beautiful Ideas

Welsh baby names have a rich heritage and beautiful meanings. Here are some ideas.

## Table of Contents

1. [Popular Welsh Girl Names](#s1)
2. [Popular Welsh Boy Names](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Popular Welsh Girl Names {#s1}

Cerys (love), Eleri (river), Ffion (foxglove), Nia (brightness), Seren (star), Cariad (darling), Bethan (consecrated)

## Popular Welsh Boy Names {#s2}

Dylan (sea), Ieuan (God is gracious), Rhys (enthusiasm), Tomos (twin), Gethin (dark-skinned), Owain (young warrior), Elis (God is my oath)

## FAQ {#faq}

### What do Welsh names mean?

Welsh names often relate to nature, rivers, and qualities. Seren means star, Dylan means sea, Cerys means love. They're beautiful and meaningful choices.
