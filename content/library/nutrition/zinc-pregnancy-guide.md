# Zinc in Pregnancy: Why It Matters

Zinc is essential for your baby's growth and immune system development during pregnancy.

## Table of Contents

1. [Why Zinc Matters](#s1)
2. [Food Sources](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Why Zinc Matters {#s1}

Zinc supports: cell growth, DNA synthesis, immune function, and wound healing. Your baby needs zinc for normal development. The NHS recommends 7mg/day in pregnancy.

## Food Sources {#s2}

Rich sources include: meat, shellfish, legumes, seeds, nuts, dairy, and whole grains. Most pregnancy vitamins include zinc.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Can I take zinc supplements in pregnancy?

Yes, at recommended doses (7-15mg/day). Most prenatal vitamins contain adequate zinc. Avoid high doses without medical supervision.
