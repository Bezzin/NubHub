# Gummy vs Tablet Prenatal Vitamins

Gummy vitamins are appealing if you struggle with tablets, especially with morning sickness. But are they as good?

## Table of Contents

1. [Gummy Pros and Cons](#s1)
2. [Tablet Pros and Cons](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Gummy Pros and Cons {#s1}

Gummies are easier to take and gentler on the stomach. However, they often contain less iron, may have added sugar, and typically don't include calcium. Check the label carefully.

## Tablet Pros and Cons {#s2}

Tablets usually contain the full range of nutrients at proper doses. They may be harder to swallow and can worsen nausea. Some women switch to gummies temporarily during morning sickness.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Are gummy prenatal vitamins as effective?

They can be, but check the label. Many gummies lack adequate iron and calcium. If using gummies, you may need separate iron and calcium supplements.
