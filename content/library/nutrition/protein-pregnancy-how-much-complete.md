# Protein Pregnancy How Much Complete

How much protein do you need in pregnancy? Complete guide to protein requirements and sources.

## Overview

Protein is essential for your baby's growth. Here's how much you need and where to get it.

## FAQ

### The NHS recommends increasing protein intake in pregnancy to approximately 70-100g per day (depending on your weight). Good sources: lean meat, poultry, fish, eggs, dairy, legumes, nuts, seeds, and tofu. Include protein at every meal. Most women in the UK get enough protein without needing supplements.

How much protein do I need in pregnancy?

### Approximately 70-100g per day, depending on your weight. This is about 25g more than pre-pregnancy. Include a source of protein at every meal — eggs, meat, fish, beans, or dairy.
