# Calcium Rich Foods Pregnancy Complete

Complete list of calcium-rich foods for pregnancy. How to get enough calcium for your baby's bones.

## Overview

Calcium is essential for your baby's bone and teeth development. Here's how to get enough.

## FAQ

### Calcium-rich foods: dairy (milk, cheese, yogurt), fortified plant milks, leafy greens (kale, spinach), tofu (calcium-set), sardines (with bones), dried figs, and fortified cereals. The NHS recommends 700mg of calcium per day in pregnancy. Most women get enough from a balanced diet.

How much calcium do I need in pregnancy?

### The NHS recommends 700mg of calcium per day in pregnancy. This is the same as before pregnancy. Most women get enough from a balanced diet including dairy or fortified alternatives.
