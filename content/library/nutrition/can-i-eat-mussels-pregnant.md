## Is Mussels Safe in Pregnancy? {#is-it-safe}

Mussels is a shellfish that is safe to eat during pregnancy when properly prepared and stored.

The main concerns with food in pregnancy are listeria (a bacteria that can cause serious illness), toxoplasmosis (a parasite), salmonella (a bacteria), and vitamin A toxicity.

## NHS Guidance on Mussels {#nhs-guidance}

The NHS advises that yes - cook thoroughly until shells open. Always follow NHS food safety guidance during pregnancy.

## How to Eat Mussels Safely {#how-to-eat-safely}

Mussels is safe to eat during pregnancy. Follow general food hygiene: check use-by dates, store at correct temperature, and prepare cleanly.

## Frequently Asked Questions {#faq}

### What if I accidentally ate mussels while pregnant?

Don't panic. The risk of infection is low. Watch for symptoms like fever, nausea, vomiting, or diarrhoea. If you feel unwell, contact your GP or call 111.
