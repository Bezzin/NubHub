## Is Energy Drinks Safe in Pregnancy? {#is-it-safe}

Energy Drinks is a drinks that should be avoided during pregnancy due to the risk of food poisoning or other complications.

The main concerns with food in pregnancy are listeria (a bacteria that can cause serious illness), toxoplasmosis (a parasite), salmonella (a bacteria), and vitamin A toxicity.

## NHS Guidance on Energy Drinks {#nhs-guidance}

The NHS advises that avoid - high caffeine and sugar content. Always follow NHS food safety guidance during pregnancy.

## How to Eat Energy Drinks Safely {#how-to-eat-safely}

Energy Drinks should be avoided during pregnancy. If you want a similar taste, try safer alternatives like hard cheeses, pasteurised products, or thoroughly cooked options.
