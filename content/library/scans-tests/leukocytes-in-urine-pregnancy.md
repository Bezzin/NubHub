# Leukocytes In Urine Pregnancy

Leukocytes (white blood cells) in urine during pregnancy — what it means and when to get treatment.

## Overview

Leukocytes in your urine sample means white blood cells are present, which can indicate an infection.

## FAQ

### Leukocytes can indicate: a urinary tract infection (UTI), a kidney infection, or contamination from vaginal discharge. Your midwife may send the sample for culture to check for bacteria. If infection is confirmed, you'll be prescribed pregnancy-safe antibiotics.

Do leukocytes always mean infection?

### Not always. Sometimes the sample is contaminated. If leukocytes are found, your midwife may ask for a clean-catch sample and possibly send it for culture.
