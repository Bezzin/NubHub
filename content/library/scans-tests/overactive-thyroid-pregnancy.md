# Overactive Thyroid Pregnancy

Overactive thyroid (hyperthyroidism) in pregnancy — symptoms, treatment, and management.

## Overview

An overactive thyroid during pregnancy requires careful management. Here's what you need to know.

## FAQ

### Hyperthyroidism in pregnancy is less common than hypothyroidism. Symptoms include weight loss, rapid heartbeat, and anxiety. Treatment depends on severity — some medications are safe in pregnancy. Untreated hyperthyroidism can cause complications including preterm birth.

How is overactive thyroid treated in pregnancy?

### With antithyroid medication. Propylthiouracil (PTU) is preferred in the first trimester. Your endocrinologist will monitor your levels closely and adjust medication as needed.
