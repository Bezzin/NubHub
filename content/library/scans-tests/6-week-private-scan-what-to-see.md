# 6-Week Private Scan: What You'll See

A 6-week private scan can provide early reassurance. Here's what you can expect to see.

## What You'll See at 6 Weeks

At 6 weeks, the scan should show:
- The gestational sac
- The yolk sac
- Possibly the fetal pole
- Possibly a heartbeat (just starting)

## FAQ

### Is 6 weeks too early for a private scan?

6 weeks is on the early side. You may see a heartbeat, but if the dates are slightly off, you might only see a sac. Many clinics recommend waiting until 7-8 weeks.

### Will I see a heartbeat at 6 weeks?

Possibly. The heartbeat typically starts around 6 weeks but may not be visible until 7 weeks. If you don't see one, don't panic - it may just be too early.
