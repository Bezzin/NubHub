# When Can You See the Heartbeat on Ultrasound?

Seeing your baby's heartbeat on ultrasound is one of the most emotional moments of early pregnancy. Here's when you can expect to see it.

## When the Heartbeat Appears

- **5-6 weeks**: Heartbeat may be visible with transvaginal ultrasound
- **6-7 weeks**: Heartbeat usually visible with transvaginal ultrasound
- **7-8 weeks**: Heartbeat clearly visible on most scans
- **8+ weeks**: Heartbeat clearly visible on abdominal ultrasound

## What's a Normal Heart Rate?

- 6 weeks: 90-110 bpm
- 7 weeks: 120-160 bpm
- 8 weeks: 150-170 bpm
- 9-12 weeks: 160-180 bpm

## FAQ

### What if there's no heartbeat at 6 weeks?

It may simply be too early. A follow-up scan at 7-8 weeks usually shows a heartbeat. Don't panic if it's not visible at 6 weeks.

### Does seeing a heartbeat guarantee a healthy pregnancy?

Seeing a heartbeat greatly reduces miscarriage risk (to about 2-5%), but it doesn't guarantee a pregnancy will continue. However, it's a very positive sign.
