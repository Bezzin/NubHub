# CRL (Crown-Rump Length) Explained

CRL stands for Crown-Rump Length. It's the measurement from the top of your baby's head to the bottom of their buttocks, taken during an ultrasound scan.

## What CRL Tells You

CRL is used to:
- Determine your baby's gestational age
- Confirm your due date
- Check that your baby is growing as expected

## FAQ

### What's a normal CRL at 12 weeks?

At 12 weeks, the average CRL is approximately 55-60mm. Your sonographer will compare your baby's CRL to standard growth charts.

### Can CRL predict the due date?

Yes. In the first trimester, CRL is the most accurate way to determine your due date. It's more accurate than calculating from your last menstrual period.
