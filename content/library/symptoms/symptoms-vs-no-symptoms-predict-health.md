## FAQ

### Having strong pregnancy symptoms does NOT guarantee a healthy pregnancy, and having NO symptoms does NOT mean something is wrong. Studies show no reliable correlation between symptom intensity and pregnancy outcomes. Some women with severe symptoms miscarry, and some with no symptoms have healthy pregnancies. The only way to know is through scans.

Do strong symptoms mean a healthy pregnancy?

### Not necessarily. While some studies suggest a link between morning sickness and lower miscarriage risk, many women with no symptoms have healthy pregnancies. Symptoms don't reliably predict outcomes.
