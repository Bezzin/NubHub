# Pregnancy Superfoods: Complete List

These nutrient-packed foods are especially beneficial during pregnancy.

## Top Pregnancy Superfoods

- Eggs: choline, protein, vitamin D
- Salmon (cooked): omega-3, vitamin D
- Spinach: folate, iron, calcium
- Sweet potatoes: vitamin A (as beta-carotene), fibre
- Lentils: protein, iron, folate
- Yogurt: calcium, protein, probiotics
- Berries: antioxidants, vitamin C
- Avocado: healthy fats, folate, potassium
- Nuts: protein, healthy fats, magnesium
- Oats: fibre, iron, B vitamins

## FAQ

### What are the best foods to eat in pregnancy?

Focus on: eggs, cooked salmon, leafy greens, lentils, yogurt, berries, nuts, and whole grains. These provide the nutrients your baby needs for development.

### Are there foods that boost baby's brain development?

Yes. Eggs (choline), cooked salmon (DHA), and walnuts (omega-3) are particularly beneficial for brain development. Include these regularly.
