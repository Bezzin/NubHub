# No Symptoms at 5 Weeks: Is It Normal?

At 5 weeks, many women have no symptoms. This is completely normal.

## Table of Contents

1. [What's Happening at 5 Weeks](#s1)
2. [When Symptoms May Start](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## What's Happening at 5 Weeks {#s1}

At 5 weeks, hCG is rising but may not be high enough to cause symptoms yet. The embryo is implanting and the placenta is starting to form.

## When Symptoms May Start {#s2}

Most women start noticing symptoms between 6-8 weeks as hCG levels rise. At 5 weeks, no symptoms is the norm rather than the exception.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is it normal to have no symptoms at 5 weeks?

Yes. At 5 weeks, hormone levels are still low and many women feel completely normal. Symptoms typically start at 6-8 weeks.
