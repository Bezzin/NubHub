# Symptoms Disappearing at 9 Weeks

Symptoms fading around 9 weeks can be alarming but is often completely normal.

## Table of Contents

1. [Why Symptoms May Ease at 9 Weeks](#s1)
2. [When to Worry](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Why Symptoms May Ease at 9 Weeks {#s1}

- hCG peaks around 8-10 weeks and then starts to decline
- The placenta begins to take over hormone production
- Your body may be adjusting to hormone levels

## When to Worry {#s2}

If symptoms disappear gradually, it's usually fine. If they disappear suddenly (overnight), contact your midwife for reassurance. A scan can confirm everything is OK.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is it normal for symptoms to disappear at 9 weeks?

Yes. hCG peaks around 8-10 weeks and then starts to decline, which can cause symptoms to ease. This is a normal part of pregnancy progression.
