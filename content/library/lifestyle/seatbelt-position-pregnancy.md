# Seatbelt Position in Pregnancy: Safety Guide

Wearing a seatbelt correctly during pregnancy is essential for your safety and your baby's.

## Table of Contents

1. [Correct Position](#s1)
2. [Tips](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Correct Position {#s1}

The lap belt should go UNDER your bump, across your upper thighs — never across your belly. The diagonal belt goes between your breasts and over your shoulder. Never put the belt under your arm.

## Tips {#s2}

- Adjust your seat position as your bump grows
- Sit as far back from the steering wheel as comfortable
- Never travel without a seatbelt, even when pregnant
- If driving becomes uncomfortable, consider being a passenger

## FAQ {#faq}

### Can a seatbelt harm my baby?

No. Wearing a seatbelt correctly is much safer than not wearing one. The belt should go under your bump, not across it. In a crash, the seatbelt protects both you and your baby.
