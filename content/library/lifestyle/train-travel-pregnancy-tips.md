# Train Travel in Pregnancy: Tips and Advice

Train travel is generally safe and comfortable during pregnancy. Here are some tips.

## Table of Contents

1. [Tips](#s1)
2. [Tips](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Tips {#s1}

- Book a seat near the toilet
- Stand up and walk around every 30 minutes
- Stay hydrated
- Pack snacks in case of nausea
- Request assistance if needed — stations offer help for pregnant women
- Travel off-peak when possible for more space

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is train travel safe in early pregnancy?

Yes, train travel is safe throughout pregnancy. It's often more comfortable than driving or flying. Just stay hydrated and move around regularly.
