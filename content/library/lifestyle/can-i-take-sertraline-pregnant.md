# Can I Take Sertraline When Pregnant?

Can you take sertraline when pregnant? Here's what you need to know.

## Quick Answer

**Sertraline in pregnancy:** Sertraline is one of the most commonly prescribed antidepressants in pregnancy. It is generally considered safe, with low risk to the baby. Always discuss with your GP before starting or stopping.

## Is Sertraline Safe in Pregnancy? {#is-it-safe}

Sertraline is one of the most commonly prescribed antidepressants in pregnancy. It is generally considered safe, with low risk to the baby. Always discuss with your GP before starting or stopping.

Always consult your GP, midwife, or pharmacist before taking ANY medication during pregnancy. This includes over-the-counter medicines, herbal remedies, and supplements.

## NHS Guidance {#nhs-guidance}

The NHS advises that you should always check with a healthcare professional before taking medication in pregnancy. Some medicines can harm your baby, while others are perfectly safe.

## Alternatives {#alternatives}

If sertraline is not recommended, ask your GP or pharmacist about pregnancy-safe alternatives. There are often alternative treatments that are safe during pregnancy.

## Frequently Asked Questions {#faq}

### What if I already took sertraline before knowing I was pregnant?

Don't panic. Taking a small amount of most medications before you knew you were pregnant is unlikely to cause harm. Contact your GP or midwife for reassurance and mention it at your booking appointment.

### Can I buy sertraline over the counter while pregnant?

Always tell the pharmacist you're pregnant before buying any medication. They can advise whether it's safe and suggest alternatives if needed.
