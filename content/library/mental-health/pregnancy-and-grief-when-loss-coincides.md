# Pregnancy And Grief When Loss Coincides

How to cope when you're pregnant and grieving a loss — a loved one, a previous baby, or a miscarriage.

## Overview

Being pregnant while grieving is emotionally complex. Here's how to cope.

## FAQ

### Grief during pregnancy can be caused by: losing a loved one, a previous miscarriage, pregnancy after stillbirth, or any other loss. You may feel guilt for being happy about the pregnancy while grieving. Both emotions are valid. Seek support from your midwife, a counsellor, or a grief charity like Cruse Bereavement Support.

Is it normal to grieve during pregnancy?

### Yes. If you've experienced a loss, grief doesn't pause because you're pregnant. You may experience complex emotions — joy about the pregnancy and grief about the loss simultaneously. Both are valid.
