## Which Should You Choose? {#section-2}

NIPT (Harmony, Panorama) costs £150-£500 and screens for chromosomal conditions plus gender. SneakPeek costs £30-£80 and only tests for gender. Both are available from 9-10 weeks.

## FAQ {#faq}

### What's the most accurate gender test at 9 weeks?

NIPT (Harmony, Panorama) at 99%+ accuracy. It costs £150-£500 but also screens for chromosomal conditions. SneakPeek is cheaper but less accurate.

### Is NIPT worth it at 9 weeks?

If you want early, highly accurate gender prediction and chromosomal screening, yes. If you only want gender and are on a budget, nub theory at 12 weeks is free.
