# Pregnancy Cravings and Gender: Is There a Link?

The old wives' tale says craving sweet foods means girl, salty means boy. But is there any truth to it?

## Table of Contents

1. [The Theory](#s1)
2. [The Reality](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## The Theory {#s1}

Sweet cravings = girl, salty cravings = boy. Some versions add: citrus = girl, protein = boy.

## The Reality {#s2}

Cravings are caused by hormonal changes and nutritional needs, not fetal sex. There's no scientific link between cravings and gender. Accuracy: approximately 50%.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Do cravings predict baby gender?

No. Cravings are caused by hormones and nutritional needs, not fetal sex. This old wives' tale is approximately 50% accurate — the same as guessing.
