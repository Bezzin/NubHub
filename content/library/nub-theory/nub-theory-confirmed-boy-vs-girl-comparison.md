# Nub Theory: Confirmed Boy vs Girl Comparison

Want to see the difference between confirmed boy and confirmed girl nub theory scans? Here's a detailed comparison.

2. [Confirmed Girl Nub](#s2)

## Confirmed Boy Nub {#s1}

A confirmed boy nub shows: a clear 30+ degree upward angle from the spine, a stacked or forked tip, an elongated appearance, and often a visible caudal notch. These features are unmistakable at 12+ weeks.

## Confirmed Girl Nub {#s2}

A confirmed girl nub shows: a nub parallel to the spine or pointing slightly downward, three short flat lines, a short and flush appearance, and no stacked or forked tip. The nub blends into the body contour.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Can I compare my scan to confirmed examples?

Yes! Comparing your scan to confirmed boy and confirmed girl examples is one of the best ways to learn nub theory. Look at the angle first, then secondary features.
