# Gender Prediction Apps: Do Any Work?

There are many gender prediction apps on the market. Here's our review of whether any actually work.

## Table of Contents

1. [Nub Theory Apps](#s1)
2. [Old Wives' Tales Apps](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## Nub Theory Apps {#s1}

Some apps claim to analyse your scan photo using AI to determine the nub angle. These vary in quality. Some are reasonable; others are essentially random. None are as good as a trained human reader.

## Old Wives' Tales Apps {#s2}

Apps that use Chinese gender charts, heart rate, or cravings are all approximately 50% accurate — the same as guessing. They're fun but not predictive.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Do AI nub theory apps work?

Some AI nub theory apps show promise, but their accuracy varies widely. They're generally not as reliable as an experienced human reader. Use them for fun, not for definitive predictions.

### What's the most reliable way to predict gender?

NIPT (99%+ from 10 weeks), nub theory at 13 weeks (92-95%), and the 20-week anatomy scan (95-99%) are the most reliable methods. Apps and old wives' tales are approximately 50%.
