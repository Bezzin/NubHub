# Heart Rate Gender Prediction: Is It Accurate?

The theory says a fetal heart rate above 140 bpm means girl, below 140 means boy. But does it work?

## Table of Contents

1. [The Theory](#s1)
2. [The Reality](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## The Theory {#s1}

According to this old wives' tale: heart rate above 140 bpm = girl, below 140 bpm = boy.

## The Reality {#s2}

Studies have shown no significant difference in fetal heart rate between boys and girls. Normal fetal heart rate is 120-180 bpm regardless of sex. Accuracy: approximately 50%.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Can heart rate predict my baby's gender?

No. Studies show no difference in fetal heart rate between boys and girls. This is an old wives' tale with approximately 50% accuracy.
