# Nub Theory: Ambiguous Nub Photos

Sometimes the nub isn't clearly boy or girl. Here's what to do with an ambiguous nub.

## Table of Contents

1. [What Makes a Nub Ambiguous](#s1)
2. [What to Do](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## What Makes a Nub Ambiguous {#s1}

An ambiguous nub is at 10-30 degrees — not clearly up (boy) and not clearly flat (girl). Other factors: poor image quality, baby in wrong position, scan too early (before 12 weeks).

## What to Do {#s2}

Wait 1-2 weeks and get another scan. Get multiple opinions from nub theory communities. Consider NIPT for certainty. Accept the uncertainty — sometimes you just can't tell.

## FAQ {#faq}

### What if my nub is at 15 degrees?

A 15-degree nub is genuinely ambiguous. It could be either sex. At this angle, accuracy is approximately 50%. Wait for a later scan or consider NIPT for definitive results.
