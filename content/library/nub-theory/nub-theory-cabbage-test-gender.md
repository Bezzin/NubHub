# The Cabbage Test for Gender Prediction

## Which Should You Choose? {#section-2}

The test involves boiling red cabbage, mixing the water with your urine, and checking the colour change. Pink/red supposedly means boy, purple means girl. The result is random.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is the cabbage gender test accurate?

No. The cabbage test has no scientific basis and is approximately 50% accurate. The colour change is due to urine pH, not fetal sex.

### Are any home gender tests accurate?

NIPT and SneakPeek (blood tests) are the only home-accessible tests with real accuracy. Urine-based tests like baking soda and cabbage are old wives' tales with 50% accuracy.
