# Carrying High vs Low: Does It Predict Gender?

The old wives' tale says carrying high means girl and low means boy. But does it work?

## Table of Contents

1. [The Theory](#s1)
2. [The Reality](#s2)
3. [FAQ](#faq)

## The Theory {#s1}

According to the tale: high bump = girl, low bump = boy. The theory suggests girls sit higher in the uterus.

## The Reality {#s2}

Bump position is determined by: your muscle tone, the number of previous pregnancies, your body shape, and the baby's position. It has nothing to do with gender. Accuracy: approximately 50%.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Does carrying high or low mean boy or girl?

No. Bump position is determined by your muscle tone, body shape, and baby's position — not gender. This old wives' tale is approximately 50% accurate.
