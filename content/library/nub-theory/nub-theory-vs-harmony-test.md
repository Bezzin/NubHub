# Nub Theory vs Harmony Test

The Harmony test is a brand of NIPT that can determine gender with 99%+ accuracy from 10 weeks. Nub theory is free from your 12-week scan at 80-95% accuracy.

## Comparison {#section-1}

The Harmony test is a brand of NIPT that can determine gender with 99%+ accuracy from 10 weeks. Nub theory is free from your 12-week scan at 80-95% accuracy.

## Which Should You Choose? {#section-2}

Harmony is more accurate and earlier but costs £150-£500. Nub theory is free but less accurate. If cost is no issue, get Harmony. If you want free, nub theory is great at 12+ weeks.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Is the Harmony test worth it for gender?

If you want near-certain gender prediction, yes. It's 99%+ accurate from 10 weeks. But it also screens for chromosomal conditions, which many find valuable.

### Can I use both nub theory and Harmony?

Yes! Get Harmony at 10 weeks for certainty, then try nub theory at 12 weeks for fun to see if they match.
