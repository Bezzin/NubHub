# Nub Theory: 13-Week Scan Examples

At 13 weeks, nub theory is at its most accurate (92-95%). Here's what 13-week nubs look like.

## 13-Week Boy Nubs {#s1}

At 13 weeks, a boy nub is unmistakable: clearly angled at 30+ degrees, often stacked or forked, elongated, and pointing clearly upward. The penis is beginning to form.

## 13-Week Girl Nubs {#s2}

At 13 weeks, a girl nub is completely flat, parallel to the spine, with three short lines. There's no upward angle at all. The clitoris is formed.

## FAQ {#faq}

### Why is 13 weeks the best time for nub theory?

At 13 weeks, the genital tubercle has reached maximum differentiation. Boy nubs are at their most angled, girl nubs are completely flat. This gives 92-95% accuracy — the highest of any ultrasound-based method.
