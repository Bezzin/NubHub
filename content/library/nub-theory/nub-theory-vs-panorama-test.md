## FAQ {#faq}

### Is Panorama better than Harmony?

Both are highly accurate NIPT brands. Panorama can be done from 9 weeks vs 10 for Harmony. The choice usually comes down to what your clinic offers.

### Does Panorama test for gender?

Yes. Panorama can determine fetal sex with 99%+ accuracy as part of the test.
